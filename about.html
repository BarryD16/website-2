About Orchestrate Digital

We're on a mission to make AI and digital technology work for businesses in the North East of England — practically, accessibly, and on your terms.

Our Story

Born in the North East of England, built for Local businesses

Orchestrate Digital was founded with a simple belief: that the benefits of AI and digital technology shouldn't only be available to big corporations with large IT teams. Small and medium-sized businesses deserve exactly the same access to these powerful tools — and the support to use them well.

We saw too many North East England businesses feeling left behind by the pace of technological change — overwhelmed by the choices, unsure where to start, and worried about getting it wrong. We set out to change that.

Our approach is practical, jargon-free, and always tailored to the specific needs and circumstances of the businesses we work with. We meet you where you are, face to face, and help you take the right steps forward.

Our Coverage Area

Where We Work

Based in the North East England, we work with businesses right across the region — from Northumberland in the north down through Tyne & Wear, County Durham and Teesside. We also serve businesses in North Yorkshire and Cumbria.

We believe that by helping SMEs across this broad northern region embrace AI and digital technology, we can contribute to economic growth, job creation, and improved competitiveness for everyone.

We understand the specific challenges facing businesses here — because we're part of this community too, and genuinely invested in its future. Online support means we can help wherever you are.
