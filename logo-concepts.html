The Harmonics

In music theory, a rich complex tone is made up of many overlapping sine waves — a fundamental frequency plus harmonics. Each layer adds depth and character. This logo shows exactly that: five layered waveforms in different colours, each representing a different part of the orchestra (or business function), combining into one powerful composite waveform.

Concept 05
The Crescendo

The crescendo hairpin (‹) is the most iconic symbol in musical notation — it means: build from quiet to powerful, from one to many, from simple to complex. This mark shows that exact journey: a single point of origin, expanding into many fine lines, each growing bolder, until the full force of every part is heard together. It marks both the musical notation and the journey of digital transformation.
